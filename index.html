About

Hi! I am a fourth year Honours Integrated Science student at McMaster University.

My program, Integrated Science, is an interdisciplinary, research-based program, which aims to develop the next generation of scientists. I've had the opportunity to explore a wide range of research topics, from space exploration, to energy resources, to cancer and drug development. I've also had the chance to explore different science fields by concentrating my elective courses in neuroscience, math, and physics. My career goal is to pursue research in computational neuroscience.
